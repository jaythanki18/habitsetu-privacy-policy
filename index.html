01 — Overview

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

This Privacy Policy describes how HabitSetu ("we," "our," or "the app") handles information when you use our Android application. We are committed to being transparent about our data practices.

This app does not require account registration or login. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information as part of normal app usage.

Plain language summary: Your habit data stays on your device. The only external data sharing occurs through Google AdMob (for displaying ads) and when you voluntarily contact our support team.
02 — Data We Collect

Information Collection & Use

Habit Data (Local Only)

All habit information you create, edit, or delete — including habit names, schedules, and completion history — is stored exclusively in a local database on your device using Room Database. This data never leaves your device under normal app operation and is never transmitted to our servers.

Device Information (Support Only — Voluntary)

When you tap "Contact Us," the following technical information is collected and included in your support request to help us diagnose issues:

  • App Version and Build Number
  • Device Model and Manufacturer
  • Android OS Version
  • Android SDK Version
  • Exported copy of your local habit database

This information is collected only when you explicitly initiate a support request. It is used solely for debugging and improving app functionality.

Advertising Data (via Google AdMob)

Our app displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may automatically collect certain data to serve relevant ads. See Section 4 for full details.

03 — App Permissions

Device Permissions Explained

Our app requests the following Android permissions. Each permission is used only for the specific purpose described below:

Permission Purpose
POST_NOTIFICATIONS To send you habit reminders and alarm notifications at your scheduled times.
SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM To schedule precise habit reminders at the exact times you define.
RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED To automatically restore your scheduled habit alarms after your device is restarted.
INTERNET Used solely to load and display advertisements via Google AdMob.

No permissions are used to collect, track, or transmit personal information beyond the stated purposes above.

04 — Advertising

Google AdMob & Advertisements

Our app uses Google AdMob to display interstitial advertisements. AdMob is a third-party advertising service provided by Google LLC.

Data AdMob May Collect
  • Advertising ID (Google Advertising Identifier / GAID)
  • Device identifiers and hardware information
  • IP address (used to derive approximate location)
  • App usage and interaction data
  • Diagnostic information

AdMob's data collection and use is governed entirely by Google's Privacy Policy. We have no control over, and are not responsible for, the data practices of Google AdMob.

Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
EU / EEA Users — Personalized Ads & Consent

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you will be presented with a consent dialog when you first launch the app. This dialog allows you to choose whether to receive personalized or non-personalized advertisements, in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Personalized ads will only be shown if you provide explicit consent. If you decline, non-personalized advertisements will be displayed instead. You may update your consent preferences at any time through your device settings.

Opt-Out of Personalized Ads

You can opt out of interest-based advertising at any time by visiting your device's ad settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Delete advertising ID" or "Opt out of Ads Personalization"
05 — Data Sharing

How We Share Information

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. The limited data sharing that occurs is as follows:

Recipient Data Shared Condition
Google AdMob Device/ad identifiers, usage signals Automatically during ad display
Support Team Device info + habit database export Only when YOU tap "Contact Us"
Any other party None Never

We do not operate any external servers or cloud infrastructure. No habit data is ever transmitted to us automatically.

06 — Data Storage & Security

How Your Data Is Stored

All app data — including your habits, schedules, and preferences — is stored locally on your device using Android's Room Database (SQLite). This means:

  • Your data exists only on your physical device
  • We do not operate any servers, databases, or cloud storage
  • Your data is protected by your device's built-in security (PIN, biometrics, encryption)
  • Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all associated data from your device
Note: Because all data is stored locally, we cannot recover your data if your device is lost, reset, or if you uninstall the app. We recommend backing up your device regularly.
07 — GDPR & EU Users

European Privacy Rights

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have specific rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and equivalent local laws.

Your GDPR Rights
  • Right of Access — You may request a copy of any data we hold about you
  • Right to Erasure — You may request deletion of your data at any time
  • Right to Withdraw Consent — You may withdraw advertising consent at any time via device settings
  • Right to Object — You may object to the processing of your data for advertising purposes

Since we do not store personal data on our servers, most of these rights can be exercised directly on your device by clearing app data or uninstalling the application. For advertising-related rights, please refer to Google's privacy controls.

The legal basis for processing your data through AdMob in the EEA is your explicit consent, obtained through the in-app consent dialog upon first launch.

08 — Children's Privacy

Children Under 13

Our app is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used our app or provided personal information, please contact us immediately at the address below and we will take appropriate steps to address the situation.

Our advertising implementation does not use child-directed ad settings as this app is intended for general audiences aged 13 and above.

09 — Your Rights

Managing & Deleting Your Data

Delete All App Data

You can delete all data associated with this app at any time using either of these methods:

  • Uninstall the app — Permanently removes all local data from your device
  • Clear app data — Settings → Apps → Habit Tracker → Storage → Clear Data
Manage Ad Preferences
  • Android Settings → Google → Ads → Delete Advertising ID
  • Visit adssettings.google.com to manage Google ad personalization

Since no account or login exists in our app, there is no account deletion process required.
